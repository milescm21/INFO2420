Web Site Critiques

www.utah.com/nationalparks/

  1. 1. What was your first impression of this site?
  2. 2. How does this site organize the flow of information? (Do they have links on the left and test on the right? Some other scheme? No scheme?)
  3. 3. Does the site structure make sense or does it confuse you? Why?
  4. 4. Does the site graphics/layout/multimedia enhanse the site or detract from it? Why?
  5. 5. How long did it take for the home page to load? Was the load time reasonable?
  6. 6. Was the text color and size readable? If a background color or image was used, did it enhance or detract from the site?
  7. 7. Did the site have a navigation bar or other navigation tool? Was the navigation well done?
  8. 8. Did the site have a color scheme or look and feel that was repeated on all the pages?
  9. 9. How does the site respond to smaller window sizes (responsive design)?
  10. 10. What does the site offer that was unusual or very well done?
